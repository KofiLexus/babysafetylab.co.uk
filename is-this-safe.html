Common safety knowledge

🍯
Honey

Not safe under 12 months — risk of infant botulism.

💊
Ibuprofen

Not under 3 months. Weight-based from 3m+ if over 5kg.

🧴
Essential Oils

Many are unsafe near babies. Eucalyptus & mint — avoid.

💊
Paracetamol

Safe from 2 months if over 4kg. Always dose by weight.

🥛
Cow's Milk (drink)

Not as main drink before 12 months. OK in food from 6m.

🐟
Fish (cooked)

Safe from 6 months. Avoid shark, swordfish, marlin.
